About Beauty and the Beast

Beauty and the Beast is a 1991 American animated musical romantic fantasy film produced by Walt Disney Feature Animation and released by Walt Disney Pictures. It tells the story of a young woman named Belle, who is taken prisoner by a Beast in his castle. Despite her fears, she befriends the castle's enchanted staff and learns to look beyond the Beast's exterior to recognize the heart and soul of the human Prince within.

About Ghost in the Shell

Ghost in the Shell is a Japanese cyberpunk science fiction media franchise created by Masamune Shirow. It focuses on the activities of the Special Operations Division of the Public Security Section 9, a fictional law enforcement division of the Japanese National Public Safety Commission, in a future where most of the world has become interconnected by a vast electronic network.
